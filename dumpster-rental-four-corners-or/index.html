Our standard dumpster rental term is 7 days, but extensions are easy. Just ask.

What can I throw away?

General junk, roofing debris, landscaping waste, you name it. Just no hazardous materials.

How does delivery and pickup work?

You point, we drop. You fill, we haul. Whether it’s a trash container rental or waste bin rental, it’s that simple.

Do I need a permit?

Only if placing the dumpster close to me on public streets. We’ll guide you through it.

How much space is needed?

A flat area about the size of a parking spot. For larger bins like a 20 yard dumpster in Four Corners, OR, clear around 60 feet for access.

Four Corners, OR Insights: Population,
Zip Codes, Influence, and Service Areas

Four Corners is a census-designated place (CDP) in Marion County, Oregon, United States, just outside the city limits of Salem but within the city's urban growth boundary. It is part of the Salem Metropolitan Statistical Area. The population of the CDP was 16,740 at the 2020 census. It is named after the intersection of State Street and Lancaster Drive.
